Summer Shorts

YRM’s Summer Institute (SI) is a five-week intensive program that employs 25 youth, providing them with hands-on work experience in community organizing and media making in order to build their sense of power, leadership capacity, and commitment to generating community change. In SI, youth learn and hone transferable skills in time management, oral and written communication, critical thinking, media literacy, teamwork, facilitation and public speaking. The program culminates in a public screening of the youth’s media productions. In the past, SI youth have they created short films and PSA’s (public service announcements) on such topics as bullying, LGBTQ acceptance, the importance of fathers, and teen pregnancy.
