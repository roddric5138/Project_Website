What do you need to stay involved?

Regardless of whether you live in or near connecticut, here are the ways to stay involved:

  • Attend regional alumni events.

  • Participate in alumni network.

  • Share your advice with fellow alumns and current students.

  • Become a mentor to an aspiring high school student.

  • Donate generously to the YRM.

Your alumni needs continuous support from alma to ensure that the academic programs provide the best curriculum to the drop out high school students. Please stay involved and play an active part in our future Programs.
